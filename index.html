Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026

minirank is a collaborative image-ranking app. This policy explains what data we handle, why, and how. We designed minirank to collect as little data as possible — and all of it is anonymous.

What we collect

When you participate in a ranking, the app generates a random, unique identifier (UUID) specific to that ranking session. We store only two things:

  • The UUID assigned to you for that ranking.
  • The rank you gave to each image in that ranking.

A new UUID is created for every ranking you participate in. We have no way to link UUIDs across different rankings or to connect any UUID to you, your device, or your Apple ID. The data we collect is anonymous by design.

What we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, IP address, device identifier, location, contacts, or any other personal information. We do not use cookies, fingerprinting, or any other tracking technology to identify you across rankings or sessions.

How we collect it

All data is collected directly through the interaction between the minirank app on your device and our backend servers when you submit a ranking. There is no other source of data collection.

Why we collect it

Ranking data is the core of how minirank works — it is needed to calculate and display collaborative ranking results. We do not collect any data beyond what is strictly necessary to provide this service.

Third-party services

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party. The only external service involved is Apple's built-in app analytics, which Apple manages under its own Privacy Policy. We do not have access to any data that identifies individual users through Apple's analytics.

Data retention

Ranking data (UUID and associated ranks) is retained for as long as the ranking it belongs to exists. Since this data is anonymous and cannot be linked to any individual, there is no personal data to delete.

Data security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect the data stored on our servers. Because the data we hold is anonymous and minimal, the privacy risk in the unlikely event of a breach is extremely low.

Children's privacy

minirank does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since all data is anonymous and no personal information is collected from any user, there is no age-gated data at risk.

Your rights

Because minirank does not collect or store personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions or concerns, you are welcome to contact us.

Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and revise the effective date above. If a change is significant, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users through the app.
